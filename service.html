The stack

01 / TRACKING

Player counts, watch-time and matchmaking volume pulled straight from partner platforms, every sixty seconds, all day.

Category Intelligence

Chat volume, clip shares and sentiment folded into a single score — the closest thing to a crowd reaction, measured.

Before you ask

Common questions

Is Stackly free for players?+

Yes — every ranking, category and alert on the main site is free and always will be. Scout, Pro and Studio are for teams who want the raw API feed, not the public charts.

Where does the raw data come from?+

Session, watch-time and matchmaking data licensed directly from partner platforms across PC, console and mobile, plus publicly reported download and chart data where a partnership doesn't exist yet.

How fast does a new game start showing up?+

Once a partner integration is live, a title can appear in category rankings within one refresh cycle — sixty seconds — though Spotlight curation waits for a few days of real play data first.

Can I cancel a Pro or Studio plan anytime?+

Monthly Pro plans cancel anytime from your dashboard with no notice period. Annual Studio contracts include a standard 30-day off-ramp.

Do you sell or share individual player data?+

No. Every score is aggregated at the title level — Stackly never sells identifiable player data, only aggregate trend signal.

Ready to plug into the feed?

Talk to the partnerships team, or explore the live API reference — either way you'll be looking at real data in minutes.
